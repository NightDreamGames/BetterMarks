# üìí FEATURES

‚ñ∫ Grade and Average Tracking -- Averages automatically calculated through every Term and Subject.

‚ñ∫ Easy Grade Entry -- Enter your Grade quickly without bothering with the unnecessary stuff by
keeping the Default values.

‚ñ∫ Grade Editing -- Update or Remove your Grades at any time.

‚ñ∫ Luxemburgish School System Integration -- Easily set up the app by selecting your Class if you're
in a Luxemburgish School and avoid setting your Subjects by hand.

‚ñ∫ Custom Grading Systems -- You can completely customize the Calculation System to fit your needs.

‚ñ∫ Easy Subject Setup -- You can easily set up your Subjects with the Luxemburgish System Integration
by selecting your Class, or else by creating your own Subjects with Weights.

‚ñ∫ Rounding -- Choose how your Grades get rounded: Up, Down, To Integer, 10th, 100th, or 1000th.

‚ñ∫ Multiple Semester / Trimester / Year Support -- Select which term you want to add Grades to and
switch them at any time. You have complete flexibility and Graded handles it all.

‚ñ∫ Clean Interface -- Enjoy a clean interface, available in Light and Dark Mode.

‚ñ∫ Automatic Data Backup -- Grades automatically back up on your Google Account and get restored on
your new device.
